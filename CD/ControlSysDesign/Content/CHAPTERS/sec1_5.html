The Excitement of Control Engineering

1.5 System Integration

As is evident from the above discussion, success in control engineering depends on taking a holistic viewpoint. Some of the issues that are embodied in a typical control design include:

  • plant, i.e. the process to be controlled
  • objectives
  • sensors
  • actuators
  • communications
  • computing
  • architectures and interfacing
  • algorithms
  • accounting for disturbances and uncertainty
