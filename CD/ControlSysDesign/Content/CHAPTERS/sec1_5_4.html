The Excitement of Control Engineering

1.5.4 Actuators

Once sensors are in place to report on the "state" of a process, then the next issue is the ability to affect, or actuate, the system in order to move the process from the current state to a desired state. Thus, we see that actuation is another intrinsic element in control problems. The availability of new or improved actuators also often opens the door to significant improvements in performance. Conversely, inadequate, or poor, actuators often lie at the heart of control difficulties. Conversely, inadequate, or poor, actuators often lie at the heart of control difficulties.
