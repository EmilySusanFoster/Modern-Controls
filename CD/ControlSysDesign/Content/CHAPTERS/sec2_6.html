2.6 High gain feedback and inversion

As we will see later, typical plant models cannot be inverted exactly. We will next show, however, that there is a rather intriguing property of feedback that implicitly generates an approximate inverse of dynamic transformations, without that the inversion needing to be carried out explicitly.
