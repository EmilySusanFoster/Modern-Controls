The Excitement of Control Engineering

1.7 Further reading

Historical notes

The IEEE History Centre and its resources are an excellent source for the historically interested reader. Other useful sources are:

Black, H.W. (1934). Stabilized Feedback Amplifiers. Bell Systems Tech. Journal, 13:1-18. Black, H.

Bode, H. (1969). Feedback: the history of an idea. In Selected Papers on Mathematical Trends in Control Theory, pages 106-123. Dover, New York. Bode, H.

Fuller, A. (1976). The early development of control theory. Trans. ASME, J. Dyn. Sys. Meas. Contr., 98:109-118, 224-235. Fuller, A.

James, H.M., Nichols, N.B., and Phillips, R.S., editors (1947). Theory of Servomechanisms. McGraw-Hill, New York. James, H. Nichols, N. Phillips, R.

Maxwell, T. (1868). On governors. Proc. Royal Soc. London, 16:270-283. Maxwell, T.

Mayr, O. (1970). The Origins of Feedback Control. MIT Press, Cambridge, Mass. Mayr, O.
