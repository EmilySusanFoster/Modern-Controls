Inverted Pendulum Tutorial

System Description

The inverted pendulum control problem is the same as trying to balance a broom handle on your fingertip. You move the base of the handle to attempt to keep it upright. In control demonstrations, this is often mimicked in one direction by using a cart with an attached mass as shown in the figure below. (This photo shows a real inverted pendulum system at the University of Newcastle.)
