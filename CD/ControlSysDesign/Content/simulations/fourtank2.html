Four Coupled Tanks Case Study

Dynamics

This apparatus consists of four interconnected water tanks, two pumps and associated valves. The apparatus inputs are the supplies to the pumps, v1 and v2, (voltages) and the outputs are the water levels in the bottom two tanks, y1 and y2 (voltages). The flow to each tank is adjusted using the associated valves.
