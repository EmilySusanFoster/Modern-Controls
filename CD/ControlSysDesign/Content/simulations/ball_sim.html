System Description

The ball and plate system is somewhat similar in nature to the inverted pendulum system. The object is to balance a ball on a flat metal plate by tilting the plate to move the ball. This problem will be discussed in one dimension only.

The diagram shows the layout of the system. Motors tilt the plate via an arm attached to it, and the ball rolls in the appropriate direction under the force of gravity.

A cascaded control system will be used to control the ball and plate apparatus, as shown in the diagram below.
