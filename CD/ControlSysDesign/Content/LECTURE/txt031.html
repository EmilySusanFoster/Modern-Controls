Explanation for slide 31

The first sub-problem we look at is flatness control. This is a transverse-machine problem. The slide shows a model of the roll. The essential issue of interest here is that non-uniform roll radii can produce non-uniform flatness in the rolled product. Several control loops are typically used to address this problem. For example, water sprays are used to cool the rolls - hence differentially changing their radii across the machine.
