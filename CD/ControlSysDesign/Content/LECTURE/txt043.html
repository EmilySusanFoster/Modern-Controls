Explanation for slide 43

In the spirit of obtaining a virtual sensor for strip velocity, we examine the physical arrangement of the coiler and uncoiler (where the strip is stored on each side of the mill). As indicated in the figure, if you could measure the radius of the material on the rolls and also the angular velocity of the rolls, then their product would immediately give the required strip velocity. Actually, measuring angular velocity is easy (a simple pulse encoder will do) but measuring the radius is harder …
