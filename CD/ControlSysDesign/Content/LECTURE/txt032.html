Explanation for slide 32

One actually ends up with nonlinear multivariable controllers. Nonlinear because of the nature of heat transfer from water to rolls, multivariable because there are typically 20 or 30 sprays across the width of the rolls and their outputs strongly interact. Also there are fundamental robustness issues which limit the "spatial" bandwidth that can be achieved across the strip. Similar problems arise in other areas, e.g. paper machines.
