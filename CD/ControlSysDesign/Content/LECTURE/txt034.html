There are many other interesting issues. For example, we have called the control signals in the above discussion cooling water but, in fact, the temperature of the rolls changes with time as shown in the figure. Hence, the water starts out as heating water. (Increasing the flow of water then actually expands the rolls). Eventually, the temperature of the rolls passes the temperature of the water, at which time, the sign of the gain of the controller has to be changed. This is a highly nonlinear phenomenon that cannot be addressed by traditional robust control.
