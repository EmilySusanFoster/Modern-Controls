Explanation for slide 18

This slide shows simulation results which confirm the performance of the above design. The top left plot shows the effect on pH of a varying influent flow rate into a single tank. The bottom left plot shows the effect on pH of a sticking valve in a single tank. (Actually a very small variation in influent flow has been applied to the system so that the effect of the sticking valve can be seen.) Note that the size of the errors in these two plots are comparable. The plot on the right hand side shows the result when 3 stages of pH neutralisation are used. Notice that the final pH (blue curve) is very close to 7 despite the difficulties discussed above.
