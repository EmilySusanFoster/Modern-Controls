Explanation for slide 49

This plot shows the simulated response to an input thickness disturbance with idealized sensors and actuators. The vertical axis is exit thickness error and the horizontal axis is time. The red curve shows what we would expect to achieve given the quality of the sensors and actuators as outlined above. Alas, the actual results are given by the black line. Notice that, the response starts out well but then appears to slow up (or hold-up).
