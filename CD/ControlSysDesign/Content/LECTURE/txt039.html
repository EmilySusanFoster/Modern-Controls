Explanation for slide 39

The problem referred to above is absolutely fundamental and thus cannot be remedied - within the given architecture. However, this implies we should look for alternative architectures. One solution is to replace the (delayed) measurement of exit thickness by a virtual sensor (or observer) driven by other quantities that can be measured without delay. One such idea is called a Mass Flow Estimator. The key idea here is that conservation of mass across the roll gap means that the product of strip velocity and thickness is preserved. ( Note that the width is very nearly constant.)
