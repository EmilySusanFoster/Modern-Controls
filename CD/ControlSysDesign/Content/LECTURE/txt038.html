Explanation for slide 38

This picture shows a schematic of the centre line thickness control problem (in its simplest form). Note that there is delay between the thickness at the roll-gap and the thickness as measured by the sensor. Recall from slide 6 of this talk that the presence of a delay fundamentally inhibits ones ability to reduce sensitivity; i.e. to achieve good control. If unaddressed, this limitation would completely eradicate any benefits achievable from the provision of modern accurate sensors or modern high speed actuators.
